Welcome to Helios

Helios is the Greek god of the Sun. What better name to give our first Maker Summer project? In the Greek myths, Helios drove his chariot across the sky every day, his radiance illuminating the world. In honor of Helios and his chariot, we've decided to make our first project around the modern day chariot, the automobile. While this won't make your car bright enough to light up the world, it will bring it into the information age.
